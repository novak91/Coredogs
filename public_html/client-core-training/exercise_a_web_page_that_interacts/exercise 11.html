↓ ↑ Domestication

Dogs were domesticated from gray wolves about 15,000
years ago. They must have been very valuable to early
human settlements, for they quickly became ubiquitous
across world cultures.

↓ ↑ Origin of the name

The term "domestic dog" is generally used for both
domesticated and feral varieties. The dog may have been
the first animal to be domesticated, and has been the most
widely kept working, hunting, and pet in human history.
